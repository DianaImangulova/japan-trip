Train Journey to Hakone



The Odakyu Romance Car is Japan's most scenic train route to Hakone. This comfortable express train offers panoramic windows and reserved seating for the 85-minute journey through beautiful countryside.

Departing from Shinjuku, the train winds through residential Tokyo, suburban landscapes, and finally into the mountainous Hakone region. On clear days, you'll catch glimpses of Mount Fuji during the journey.

Yoshiike Ryokan



Yoshiike Ryokan is a traditional Japanese inn offering an authentic ryokan experience in the heart of Hakone. With its historic architecture, tatami-mat rooms, and warm hospitality, it provides the perfect introduction to Japanese inn culture.

Located in Hakone-Yumoto, this family-run ryokan has been welcoming guests for generations. The property features natural hot spring baths, traditional kaiseki dining, and beautifully appointed rooms overlooking Japanese gardens.

Tenzan Onsen



Tenzan Onsen is one of Hakone's most beloved hot spring resorts, offering outdoor and indoor baths fed by natural volcanic springs. Set in a forested valley, it provides the quintessential Japanese onsen experience.

The mineral-rich waters are said to have healing properties, perfect for relaxing tired muscles after your journey. With multiple bath types and stunning natural surroundings, Tenzan offers the perfect introduction to onsen culture.
